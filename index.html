Guess the Fruit Name

Instructions:

Welcome to "Guess the Fruit Name" game; I've thought of a single fruit, can you guess it?

Instructions:
  • Each game you are given a new fruit represented by the " _ "'s in "The Fruit" box.
  • You will have 2 x ( the # of original " _ "'s ) to correctly guess the fruit.
  • Each "guess" is completed by typing a "single" letter on your keyboard.
  • Each "letter" you guess will be tracked and recorded in the "Letter Guesses" field and your "remaining guesses" will be updated in the "Scoreboard".
  • If the "letter \ key" pressed is within the fruit, we will update the specific " _ " to show your sucess.
Screen Layout: Below you will see boxes titled:
  • "The Fruit" within this box you will seee a set of " _ "; Each " _ " represents a single letter within the word.
  • "Scoreboard" this will track your progress during the games: Wins; Losses; Remaining Attempts.
  • "Letter Guesses" we will also include a box where each "letter" guess is tracked for the length of the game.
